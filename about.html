Quality software development for nearly 17 years

What differentiates Onirban Software from other consultanting firms is our ability to “wrap” technology around customers’ business goals, not vice versa. No matter what the industry, no matter how large or small the project, understanding the larger context in which our work will bring value to your organization is key to our success.

Akm Momin, President and owner of Onirban Software Development, is your lead on all software development, providing you with a trusted point person to ensure the success of your project’s management from beginning to end.

With more than 19 years of experience in applying technology to solve business challenges, Akm Momin is known to many customers as the first guy who “gets it.” Akm Momin ramps up quickly on the objectives of even the most complex business models or operating environments, and can effectively translate your needs to his team of developers because he’s hands-on. Unlike some project managers, he doesn’t simply pass off instructions—he’s actually developing code alongside them as needed, overseeing the work to ensure you get what you need.

Akm Momin began his career as an Electrical Engineer where he gained invaluable experience in dealing with different businesses, budgets, and contractors. Even as an Engineer, he found himself constantly finding new ways to apply technology to solve customers’ problems. Customers were thrilled, and he found his passion.

After years of hands-on experience with computers, Akm Momin founded “Onirban” in 2012. Our list of satisfied customers has been growing ever since.
